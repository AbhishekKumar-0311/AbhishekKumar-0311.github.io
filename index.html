Data Analysis a.k.a EDA

The process of data analysis uses analytical and logical reasoning to gain information from the data. The main purpose of data analysis is to find meaning in data so that the derived knowledge can be used to make informed decisions. I have carried out Statistical Analysis of actual data, Metadata Analysis and Data Quality Analyses. I have created Profiling reports with SAS Dataflux Studio and Pandas, which gives a Standard Analysis of the Quality. These profiling reports are chained with benchmarking mechanism to decide the future course of action. Also during the Data Preparation, hundreds of Datasets and Libraries are analysed for their Metadata. I have implemented it through SAS Codes.

Data Preparation Data Integration

Data Prepartion forms the core of any BI and Data Science Solutions. I have been exposed to Data Preparation for the 50% of my career. It consists of different phases of Data Integration, Transformation, Cleansing, Quality enhancement & Standardization, Loading & Archiving. The data is Integrated from different DB's, Raw files & other applications to get a Unified view of the business. Due to Inconsistency in data across sources, Data Manipulation & Wrangling followed by Cleansing and Standardization forms an integral part. The Data is then loaded to Central Repo in a DB to be used later. These phases are implemented with SAS Coding with Macros, SQL, Data Integration and Dataflux Studio.

Automation Framework

In the present world, we are getting rid of manual intervention in the technological process of business. For the similar cause, I did develop a Reporting Framework which automates the daily, weekly and monthly MIS report generation. The process starts with the fetching of Data from source, followed by Data Preparation and Report generation in Excel,Pdf and SAS Datasets format and finally the delivery of Reports through mails. The main features are OS-Independent, Password-protected contents to exchange, Dynamic report creation and Automated E-Mails generation. I have also created multiple production-ready utilities as SAS Macros, batch-files and shell scripts. These are modular & plug-able.
